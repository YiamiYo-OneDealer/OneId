Three simultaneous readings — one mark (showing V3)

Keyhole

Disc head (filled ring) reads as the bow of a key. Stem below is the drop slot. Together the silhouette is an unmistakable keyhole — access, identity, authentication.

lowercase i

Oversized ring functions as the dot — deliberately large to create graphic tension, like the Identity Mark concept. Stem is the letter body below it.

O · I

Ring (donut) = the letter O. Stem = the letter I. The stroke weight of the ring wall matches the stem — they feel like a unified OI ligature, linking the mark back to OneDealer's OD DNA.
